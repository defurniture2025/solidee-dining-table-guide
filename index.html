Clearance matters

Leave around 90 cm from the table edge to walls, cabinets, or nearby furniture where possible.

This gives space to pull chairs out and walk behind seated guests. In narrower rooms, consider a slimmer rectangular table or a round table.

For open-plan homes in BSD City, Alam Sutera, Gading Serpong, or Jakarta apartments, the right shape can define the dining zone without blocking movement.

Pick the shape by how people gather

Different table shapes change conversation, movement, and the feeling of the room.

Rectangular

Flexible for family dining, restaurants, villas, and long open-plan interiors.

Round

Intimate and easy for conversation. Good for cafés, breakfast areas, and compact spaces.

Square

Useful for smaller layouts and modern symmetrical interiors, especially café seating.

Dining table questions

Quick answers before you choose a table size or start a project quotation.

What dining table size is best for 6 people?

A rectangular dining table around 160 to 180 cm long is usually comfortable, depending on chair width and room clearance.

How much space should be around a dining table?

Leave around 90 cm from the table edge to the wall or nearby furniture where possible.

Is solid wood good for restaurants and cafés?

Yes. Solid wood can be a strong choice when the right wood, finish, and maintenance routine are selected.
